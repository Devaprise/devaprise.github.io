About Devaprise

Devaprise is a young developer team that originated on KA. We have a fun and strong core of 6 people, but we have a bunch of little 'helpers' who help us with projects. Our oldest member is 19, so we are a young team. Don't underestimate us, because we have alot of experience between all of us.

Games

Our team's root are from making games. We make dozens of little JavaScript games. Play some of our games here and here.

Websites

As you can see, we make websites. :) We program in HTML, CSS, LESS, Javascript, and JQuery. We also make web apps using Meteor and Node.js.
