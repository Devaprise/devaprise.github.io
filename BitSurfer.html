Bit Surfer

How to play: Use your mouse to avoid sharks and collect coins. When the coins go to the end of the screen, you lose points. If you touch the sharks, you lose.

Note: The game on this site is the original version of Bit Surfer.

Created by Gabriele Cirulli. Based on 1024 by Veewo Studio and conceptually similar to Threes by Asher Vollmer.
