Little History

Devaprise is a young developer team that originated on Khan Academy. We have a strong core of 6 people, but we have a bunch of little 'helpers' who help us with projects. We have members from age 12 to age 19, so we are a young team. However, please do not underestimate us, because we are all experienced in all sort of areas. We as a team will continue to strive to produce top apps, games, and softwares. What matters to us the most is not our history, but our future :)
