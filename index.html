About Devaprise

Devaprise is a young developer team that originated on KA. We have a fun and strong core of 6 people, but we have a bunch of little 'helpers' who help us with projects. Our oldest member is 19, so we are a young team. Don't underestimate us, because we have a lot of experience between all of us.
